Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a specific reason the website has designated closing hours?
Indeed, the website adheres to designated closing hours to humanize data.
Why does your website stay silent on Sundays?
Sundays are sacred, a day for introspection and quiet contemplation. We believe in the power of silence to echo the profound expressions found within our art. Join us in embracing the stillness.
Why is the website closed on Sundays?
Sundays are a canvas left blank. By closing our virtual doors on this day, we invite you to explore the art of solitude and reflect on the beauty found in moments of silence. We'll be back on Monday, ready to share more inspiration.
Why does the website close after 20:00 each day?
As the clock strikes 20:00, our virtual gallery closes its doors to allow the art to breathe in the quiet of the night. We believe that darkness has its own way of revealing the essence of creativity, and we invite you to explore it with us when the sun rises again.
